## 2. Hook-Story-Offer Machine (duplicado)

- **Original:** Hook-Story-Offer Machine
- **URL:** https://writewithai.substack.com/p/hook-story-offer-machine-the-easiest
- **Likes / Fecha / Autor:** 78 / 2023-12-10
- **Análisis completo:** Ver abajo. **Aplicabilidad: 🟢 directo — método base.**

### Estructura
1. Hero + H1 + H3 italic
2. "Ahoy, Digital Writers!"
3. Hook: "secret is the element of surprise"
4. H1 "What is the Hook-Story-Offer Framework?"
5. Origin: Russell Brunson's Dotcom Secrets
6. 3 partes: Hook + Story + Offer
7. Step 1: Name The Benefit (mental clarity, sleep, freedom, etc.)
8. Step 2: Put Benefit In Unexpected Setting ("diet support at Pizza Hut")
9. Step 3: Frame As Personal Story (I/My/How I)
10. Step 4: Edit For Clarity
11. Step 5: Train ChatGPT — prompt completo 300+ líneas con rol, pasos, formato 1/3/1
12. Screenshot output: "I never snored until I lost 30 pounds"
13. Prompt adicional para añadir CTA/offer
14. Cierre con firma
15. **Sin paywall — artículo completo accesible**

### Patrones de voz notables
- Autoridad prestada: "Big Al" Schreiter's book "Hooks!"
- Método opuestos: antónimo de "losing weight" = "gaining weight at Pizza Hut"
- Comparación directa hook #1 vs #2 vs #3
- Formato 1/3/1 explicado numericamente

### Takeaway accionable
Prompt literal completo (uno de los más valiosos):
```
I am going to train you to write an Unexpected Story Hook.
# Examples: "My diet support group meets at Pizza Hut."
# Role: Act as copywriting expert 20 years
# Personality: conversational, jargon-free, spartan sentences
# Steps:
1. Ask what I want to write about
2. I give topic
3. List 7 benefits (4-6 words each)
4. I select benefit
5. Write 7 Unexpected Story Hooks
6. Ask if I want full story
7. I select hook
8. Write 75-100 word story using pattern:
   Unexpected Story Hook → Setup → Problem → Resolution → Lesson → Action steps
## Story Format: 1/3/1 rhythm
(1) Sentence 1.
(3) Sentences 2-4.
(1) Sentence 5.
...
```

### Aplicabilidad ROBOHOGAR
**Crítico.** Este prompt funciona tal cual para cualquier CTA de producto/newsletter en ROBOHOGAR. Ej: hook "Mi aspiradora me llama por mi nombre" → story de test real → offer suscribe newsletter. Copiar el prompt entero al social-content skill. El formato 1/3/1 es la voz objetivo ROBOHOGAR.

## 3. How To Write A Story-Driven Email That Sells (Storyselling)

- **Original:** How To Write A Story-Driven Email That Sells in Less Than 15 Minutes
- **URL:** https://writewithai.substack.com/p/how-to-write-a-story-driven-email
- **Likes / Fecha / Autor:** 74 / 2024-12-18 / Nicolas Cole, Dickie Bush (guest: Jim Hamilton)
- **Aplicabilidad ROBOHOGAR:** 🟢 directo — formato storyselling para emails ROBOHOGAR.

### Estructura
1. Hero + H1 + H3 "4-Step Email Storyselling Formula"
2. Intro de Jim Hamilton (8 years experience)
3. "News flash: 'How-to' information is everywhere in age of AI"
4. H1 "The 4-Step Email Storyselling Formula":
   - **STEP 1: Story** — "What do you see?" (observation)
   - **STEP 2: Lesson** — "What does it mean?"
   - **STEP 3: Pivot** — "How does it apply to your audience?"
   - **STEP 4: Call to action** — "What should they do next?"
5. Quote clave: "Show, don't tell"
6. H1 "What You'll Need to Write..." — tools (Claude Sonnet, placeholders)
7. Prompt 1: Create Your Outline (full prompt) → [PAYWALL]

### Patrones de voz notables
- Guest author frame: "Here's Jim:"
- Reframe: "people buy coaches, not coaching"
- "Show, don't tell is the golden rule"
- Personal memorable phrase: "stimulus rich, but context poor"

### Takeaway accionable
Framework 4-step Storyselling:
1. **Story**: observa algo cotidiano (gym, supermercado, cliente). Ejemplo concreto.
2. **Lesson**: ¿qué revela sobre la naturaleza humana?
3. **Pivot**: conecta al lector y a tu offer (puente)
4. **CTA**: link casual, que la historia venda

### Aplicabilidad ROBOHOGAR
**Oro puro para newsletters ROBOHOGAR.** Cada issue puede empezar con observación real (story), lesson editorial, pivot a la review/noticia de la semana, CTA al link. Encaja perfecto con el tono "cercano, informado" del brand voice. Incorporar al skill de newsletter.
