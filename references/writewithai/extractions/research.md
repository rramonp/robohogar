## 1. How To Design A Winning Customer Avatar In 15 Min

- **URL:** https://writewithai.substack.com/p/how-to-design-a-winning-customer
- **Likes / Fecha:** 75 / 2025-01-31 / Jim Hamilton guest
- **Aplicabilidad:** 🟢 directo.

### Estructura (parcialmente scraped)
1. Hero + H1 + H3 "Reading Minds For Fun & Profit"
2. Jim Hamilton intro
3. "Doing market research sucks" — reframe
4. 5 prompt flow:
   - Demographics
   - Psychographics
   - Unwanted Solutions
   - Identity Shifts
   - Day-to-Day Life
5. H1 "Prompt 1: Demographics & Psychographics" — prompt literal completo
6. Output example extenso: Pains, Fears, Frustrations, Desires, False Beliefs, Trigger Moments, Painful Questions, Core Problem, Ultimate Desire
7. Breakdown conceptual de cada categoría
8. H1 "Prompt 2: Unwanted Solutions" → [PAYWALL]

### Patrones de voz notables
- "Trigger Moments" — naming
- "Painful Questions... they would never say out loud"
- "Give the Core Problem a unique name"

### Takeaway accionable
**Prompt 1 literal:**
```
Today, you're a world-class marketing analyst...
I want you to create a customer avatar for [OFFER]. The typical customer is [DESCRIBE]. Many struggle with [PROBLEM].

Include: Demographics, Psychographics, Values, 5 examples of:
- Pains (how problems manifest in present)
- Fears (how problems manifest in future)
- Frustrations
- Desires
- False Beliefs
- Trigger Moments (events that bring pains to forefront)
- Painful Questions (questions they'd never say out loud)

Plus Core Problem (root cause) and Ultimate Desire (vision of solved future).
Give each a unique name.
Give avatar a real name.
```

### Aplicabilidad ROBOHOGAR
**Máxima prioridad.** Correr este prompt para crear 2-3 customer avatars ROBOHOGAR:
- "María, 42 años, madrileña, 2 hijos pequeños, alérgica a pelos"
- "Javier, 55, ingeniero, padres mayores en casa"
- "Laura, 35, freelance, piso 55m² en Barcelona"

Con estos avatars, cada artículo/newsletter tiene Pains/Fears/Desires específicos a los que hablar. Cambia radicalmente la calidad del copy.

## 3. 5 Ways To Use ChatGPT For Research (Elle Griffin)

- **URL:** https://writewithai.substack.com/p/5-ways-to-use-chatgpt-for-research

1. Hero + intro de Elle Griffin (The Elysian, BBC featured)

6. H1 "5 Ways"
7. Way 1: Trace An Argument Through Centuries Of Books (anarchist vs capitalist thinkers example)
8. [PAYWALL]

### Patrones de voz notables
- Metáfora sostenida "Library of Alexandria + personal librarian"
- Ejemplo concreto de uso: anarchist thinker letter exchange

### Takeaway accionable
[PAYWALL parcial] Método 1: "Sum up all of [thinker]'s work and arguments for/against it" → sigue argumento a través de historia → identifica autores para profundizar.

### Aplicabilidad ROBOHOGAR

## 8. The Cycles Exercise For Customer Research

- **URL:** https://writewithai.substack.com/p/the-cycles-exercise-for-customer

- **Aplicabilidad:** 🟢 directo.

### Takeaway
