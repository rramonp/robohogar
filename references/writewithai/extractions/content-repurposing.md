## 1. How Ev Chapman Unlocks New Ideas From Old Content (Pretend Podcast)

- **URL:** https://writewithai.substack.com/p/how-ev-chapman-unlocks-new-ideas
- **Likes / Fecha:** 75 / 2024-08-07
- **Aplicabilidad:** 🟢 directo. **Sin paywall, artículo completo.**

### Estructura (scraped completa)
1. Hero + H1
2. Introducción a Ev Chapman (Spark Note-Taking Method, Ship 30 alumna)
3. "Hey there!"
4. Problema: "We know we should remix our content" — pero está vacío, aburrido, repetitivo
5. H1 "Spark Fresh Ideas From Old Content"
6. Story personal: invitación a podcast → preguntas revelaron nuevos ángulos
7. H1 "The 'Pretend Podcast' Prompt" — prompt literal completo
8. Screenshot output: 10 preguntas generadas sobre artículo "3 Weird Things..."
9. H1 "Turn Questions Into Short-Form, Long-Form, Or Videos"
10. Lista de beneficios del método (5 bullets)
11. Cierre + firma

### Patrones de voz notables
- Guest intro framing: "Here's Ev:"
- Story-driven discovery: "invited to podcast → aha moment"
- Meta-commentary: "AI isn't doing the thinking — it's prompting me to think better"

### Takeaway accionable
**Prompt literal:**
```
You are a world class podcast host. You are intensely curious and skilled at pulling out fresh perspectives from your guests.

Your job will be to interview me on a specific topic and content that I provide.

Prepare at least 10 questions for me & present them as a list.

Do not attempt to answer them for me - that is my job.

Topic: {Insert Your topic}
Content: {Paste your content below}
```

Cada pregunta = nueva pieza de contenido (short-form, video script, LinkedIn post).

### Aplicabilidad ROBOHOGAR
**Oro puro.** Cada artículo ROBOHOGAR puede generar 10 short-form pieces via este prompt. Ev grabó 20 reels en 3h. Rafael puede:
1. Publicar review Roborock
2. Correr Pretend Podcast → 10 preguntas
3. Grabar 10 mini-videos 60s respondiendo cada una
4. Mes de contenido IG/TikTok/YouTube Shorts alimentado
