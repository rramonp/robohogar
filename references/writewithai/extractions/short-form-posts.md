## 2. Magically Turn Headlines Into Short-Form Content

- **Original:** Magically Turn Headlines Into Short-Form Content With ChatGPT
- **URL:** https://writewithai.substack.com/p/magically-turn-headlines-into-short
- **Likes / Fecha / Autor:** 103 / 2023-04-12 / Nicolas Cole & Dickie Bush
- **Aplicabilidad ROBOHOGAR:** 🟢 directo — método Lean Writing aplica a posts sociales + newsletter teasers.

### Estructura
1. Hero + H1 + H3 italic
2. "Hey there, Digital Writers!"
3. Tesis: Lean Writing = validate ideas in market before writing long-form
4. "Outlines As Content" concept
5. Step 1: "Start With A Clear Headline (Topic + FOR WHO + SO THAT)"
6. 5 iteraciones del headline (V1→V5, cada vez más específico)
7. Ejemplo detallado con real estate rental Miami
8. Step 2: Turn Into Outline As Content — "all great writing is a list"
9. Ejemplos: New Yorker, Buzzfeed, novels, all are lists
10. Step 3: Train ChatGPT — prompt literal
11. Prompt completo disponible: genera short-form (<280 chars) from headline
12. Cierre: "But ChatGPT is still buggy" — caveats
13. **Sin paywall full-read**

### Patrones de voz notables
- "Outlines as content" — reframe conceptual memorable
- Lista de 5 versiones de headline mostrando iteración
- "ChatGPT is notoriously bad at remembering..." — honesto sobre limitaciones

### Takeaway accionable
Método Lean Writing:
1. Headline iterado hasta subatomic (Topic + FOR WHO + SO THAT)
2. Test como short-form post (lista de tips/mistakes/steps)
3. Si rinde → expandir a long-form
Prompt literal:
```
I am going to train you to write short-form content (280 characters or less).
Rules:
1. Encouraging sentence
2. 3-5 succinct bullets
3. No hashtags or emojis
Give me headline, I'll write short-form based on it.
```

### Aplicabilidad ROBOHOGAR
Perfecto para el pipeline social-content skill: cada post de review puede convertirse en 5 short-form (LinkedIn/X/IG) con variantes de "lista de tips / mistakes / questions / tools". El headline iteración 5x es ejercicio para SEO.
