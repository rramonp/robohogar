## 1. The Ultimate Irresistible Offer Formula

- **URL:** https://writewithai.substack.com/p/the-ultimate-irresistible-offer-formula
- **Likes / Fecha:** 95 / 2024-06-12
- **Aplicabilidad ROBOHOGAR:** 🟢 directo — sobre todo cuando se pivote a productos/afiliados.

### Estructura (parcialmente scraped)
1. Hero + H1 + H3 "Say these words and people will buy"
2. "Hey there, Digital Writers!" + hook
3. Proof: Khalid ($16K/month ghostwriter side hustle)
4. H1 "Everything you've been taught about sales is backwards"
5. "Toilet Paper Test" (metaphor): desesperación = contexto = valor
6. Core principle: **"Customer has to care about the PROBLEM before SOLUTION"**
7. H1 "The Problem Formula" 4 steps: Specific Problem → Reason Why → Consequence → Ultimate Negative Outcome
8. Ejemplo literal (sueño / temperatura / brain fog / enfermedad crónica)
9. H1 "The Solution Formula" — mismos 4 pasos invertidos
10. H1 "The Full Ultimate Offer Formula" — template Mad Libs 8 líneas
11. Prompt "Never Say No" → [PAYWALL]

### Patrones de voz notables
- Metáfora icónica: "The Toilet Paper Test"
- Frase notecard: "The customer has to care about the problem before they can care about the solution"
- Template Mad Libs

### Takeaway accionable
**The Ultimate Offer Formula** (Mad Libs literal):
```
Most {Audience} struggle with {Problem}.
The reason is because {Tangible Reason Why}.
When this happens, {Consequence}.
Until all of a sudden, {Ultimate Negative Outcome}.
What I do is I specialize in {Service} to {Outcome}.
The reason why I recommend {Service} is because {Why}.
And the benefit of {Solving Problem} is {Benefits}.
All of which allow you to {Ultimate Positive Outcome}.
```

### Aplicabilidad ROBOHOGAR
Altamente aplicable para CTAs de afiliación Amazon:
```
La mayoría de familias españolas luchan con el tiempo libre limitado.
La razón es que pasan 4+ horas a la semana limpiando.
Cuando esto pasa, los fines de semana se evaporan.
Hasta que un día, se dan cuenta que no disfrutan en casa.
Lo que hacemos en ROBOHOGAR es recomendar el robot que YA merece la pena comprar.
La razón es que probamos cada modelo en hogares reales españoles.
El beneficio de elegir el robot correcto es recuperar 200+ horas al año.
Todo lo cual te permite pasar más tiempo con los tuyos.
```

## 4. The 8 primal forces driving your reader

- **URL:** https://writewithai.substack.com/p/the-8-primal-forces-driving-your

- **Aplicabilidad:** 🟢 directo — las 8 fuerzas son universales.

### Estructura (inferida)
Las 8 fuerzas primales (Cashvertising-based): survival, enjoyment of food, freedom from fear, sexual companionship, comfortable conditions, superiority, care for loved ones, social approval. Prompt para aplicar a tu copy. [PAYWALL probable.]

### Takeaway accionable

## 5. How To Write Targeted Sales Copy With 5 Stages Of Customer Awareness

- **URL:** https://writewithai.substack.com/p/how-to-write-targeted-sales-copy
- **Likes / Fecha:** 61 / 2023-10-15
- **Aplicabilidad:** 🟢 directo — Schwartz's 5 stages clásicos.

### Estructura (inferida)
Las 5 stages (Eugene Schwartz): Unaware → Problem Aware → Solution Aware → Product Aware → Most Aware. Contenido específico por stage. Prompt classifier. [PAYWALL probable.]

### Takeaway accionable
[PAYWALL] Copy diferente por stage. Unaware = story/curiosity. Problem Aware = amplify pain. Solution Aware = mechanism. Product Aware = proof. Most Aware = offer/discount.

### Aplicabilidad ROBOHOGAR
Directo al content calendar: clasificar cada artículo por stage objetivo. "¿Qué es un robot aspirador?" (Unaware) vs "Dreame L50 Ultra vs Roborock S8 Pro" (Product Aware). Mapear lista newsletter por estadio.
