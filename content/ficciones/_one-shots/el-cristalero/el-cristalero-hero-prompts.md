# Hero · El cristalero · 3 candidatos de prompt

**Decisión pendiente.** Generar el hero consume créditos Gemini. He preparado los 3 prompts canónicos (3 composiciones C-XX **totalmente distintas** + 3 familias diferentes I/IV/V según regla dura 2026-04-28) para que decidas cuál lanzar primero — o si quieres los 3 a ojo.

## Ejes comunes (no se tocan)

- **Paradigma:** personaje-acción-imposibilidad (default 2026-04-26 PM)
- **Modalidad:** M2 Cobalto tormenta (cristal escarchado nocturno · altura urbana · subexplotada en catálogo)
- **Estilo:** painterly book cover Penguin Modern Classics + chiaroscuro Hopper-meets-Caravaggio + foco lumínico ámbar único
- **Personaje:** limpiacristales 50+ migrante latinoamericano, mono azul, casco con barbuquejo, cinturón de arnés con dos cabos visibles, palito de carbón en mano (3 dedos sobre guante térmico fino)
- **Acción:** dibujar una rosa de carbón sobre cristal escarchado a 30+ plantas
- **Objeto-imposibilidad:** la rosa de carbón en cristal escarchado (negro mate sobre cristal helado azul-cobalto, brillo opaco como ceniza pegada al hielo)
- **Anti-triggers:** sin texto, sin caracteres asiáticos, sin neones, sin LEDs, sin glow, sin cara reconocible de figura pública, fondo del tercio superior matte plain unmarked
- **Dimensiones:** 1200×630 (--model 2 --aspect 16:9 --size 2K + crop Pillow)

## Candidato 1 — C-07 Ventana al exterior · Familia III (frame-in-frame) · A3 ángulo bajo

> **Razón:** C-07 traduce literalmente la geometría del relato — el cristal de la torre como umbral que separa al limpiacristales (afuera, colgando) del humanoide patrullero (adentro, archivando). El frame-in-frame es el subtexto material del relato. Banda no aplica (G4, no G1). Ángulo A3 bajo añade tensión vertical (planta 31).

```
A painterly book-cover illustration in the style of Penguin Modern Classics editions
(Hopper-meets-Caravaggio chiaroscuro, oil painting feel without digital sheen):

A 50+ year-old Andean Bolivian male window cleaner hangs on safety ropes outside
the 31st floor of a Madrid corporate office tower at 4:10 AM. Blue jumpsuit, white
hard hat with chinstrap, harness with two visible safety lines, fine thermal gloves.
He grips a small charcoal stick between three fingers on top of the glove and presses
it against the frosted reinforced glass of the building. He is mid-stroke, drawing
a small five-petal rose on the frost.

VIEWED FROM BELOW (low angle, A3): camera positioned at the building edge looking
diagonally upward, showing the man's body suspended in the night air with the tower's
sheer vertical glass dominating the upper two-thirds of the frame. Behind the glass,
INSIDE the empty office floor, a humanoid security patrol robot (CUSTODIA-2: matte
graphite body, 1.75m, no LEDs, no logo, plain unmarked chest plate) stands motionless
two meters from the glass, its head turned three degrees to the left, observing.
Frame-in-frame composition: glass divides the cleaner outside from the robot inside.

LIGHTING: Single warm amber light source — a ceiling pilot light inside the empty
office — casts a diagonal beam through the glass that illuminates only the rose and
the cleaner's gloved hand. Everything else is in deep cobalt-blue shadow (M2 cobalto
tormenta modality). Backlight from the storm-cobalt sky outside.

PALETTE: deep cobalt blue #1E2A3A as dominant background and shadow, frosted glass
with cyan-cobalt highlights, single warm amber #F5A623 spotlight on the rose drawing.

NO text. NO Asian characters. NO neon signs. NO LEDs. NO recognizable Madrid skyline
landmarks behind. The window is plain frosted glass with abstract cobalt city blur
beyond — no detailed urban paisaje, no Pirulí tower visible, no logos. Robot face is
matte graphite without glow, no eyes lit. Background of upper third matte plain
unmarked dark cobalt. Painterly oil-paint texture. NO digital painting sheen.

Test legibility at 120px thumbnail: silhouette of suspended man + rose + robot
behind glass must be distinguishable.
```

## Candidato 2 — C-09 Over-the-shoulder voyeur · Familia IV (frontal/OTS) · A1 lateral eye-level

> **Razón:** C-09 OTS pone al espectador en el rol del voyeur — desde detrás del hombro del humanoide CUSTODIA-2, mirando al limpiacristales al otro lado del cristal. Subtexto: el lector se convierte en el sistema que archiva. Composición opuesta a C-07 (mismo escenario, vector de mirada inverso). Banda no aplica.

```
A painterly book-cover illustration in the style of Penguin Modern Classics editions
(Hopper-meets-Caravaggio chiaroscuro, oil painting feel without digital sheen):

OVER-THE-SHOULDER COMPOSITION (C-09): camera is positioned BEHIND and SLIGHTLY ABOVE
the right shoulder of a humanoid security patrol robot (CUSTODIA-2: matte graphite
body, 1.75m, no LEDs, no logo, plain unmarked chest plate, head visible torque from
behind, three degrees turned to the left). The robot stands motionless inside an
empty corporate office floor at 4:17 AM. The frame is composed at lateral eye-level
(A1) so the viewer sees through the robot's perceptual field.

Through the reinforced frosted glass IN FRONT of the robot, ON THE OTHER SIDE, a
50+ year-old Andean Bolivian male window cleaner hangs on safety ropes. Blue jumpsuit,
white hard hat, harness with two cabos. He has just finished drawing a five-petal
rose on the frost — visible on the glass between the robot and the man. The rose is
the focal point of the composition, framed dead-center.

LIGHTING: Single warm amber spotlight (M2 cobalto tormenta with single-source
chiaroscuro) emerging from a ceiling pilot light behind the robot, casting the
robot's silhouette as a dark mass in the foreground and illuminating the rose +
the cleaner's face on the other side of the glass.

PALETTE: deep cobalt blue #1E2A3A dominant, robot silhouette near-black graphite,
frosted glass with cyan-cobalt highlights, single warm amber #F5A623 on the rose
drawing and the cleaner's face. Storm-cobalt sky behind cleaner, abstract distant
city blur — no detailed skyline.

NO text. NO Asian characters. NO neon. NO LEDs. NO recognizable Madrid landmarks.
Robot face matte graphite without glow. Cleaner's face visible but not recognizable
as any public figure. Background of upper third matte plain unmarked dark cobalt.
Painterly oil-paint texture. NO digital painting sheen.

Test 120px: robot silhouette in foreground + rose + cleaner backlit must read clearly.
```

## Candidato 3 — C-12 Color-pop saturado · Familia V (atmósfera/color-pop) · A5 extreme macro

> **Razón:** C-12 abandona el frame-in-frame y se centra en el OBJETO-IMPOSIBILIDAD aislado: la rosa de carbón en macro extremo sobre cristal escarchado, con el cleaner y el robot reducidos a presencia indirecta (silueta del cleaner reflejada en el cristal · forma del robot fuera del foco al otro lado). Más radical compositivamente. A5 extreme macro pone al espectador a 5cm de la rosa.

```
A painterly book-cover illustration in the style of Penguin Modern Classics editions
(Hopper-meets-Caravaggio chiaroscuro, oil painting feel without digital sheen):

EXTREME MACRO COMPOSITION (C-12 + A5): the camera is positioned 5cm from a piece of
frosted reinforced glass at 4:10 AM. Filling 70% of the frame, in sharp focus, is a
small five-petal charcoal rose freshly drawn on the frost — black matte charcoal
strokes on cyan-cobalt frozen glass, the texture of the carbon visible, slight smudge
where the gloved finger pressed.

Reflected ON the glass surface in the lower-left third, semi-transparent: the
50+ year-old Andean Bolivian male window cleaner's face, partial profile, with the
hard hat and the safety rope visible. He is looking at the rose he has just drawn.
Eyes amber-lit by a single cool light source.

Behind the glass, OUT OF FOCUS in the upper-right third, the matte graphite shoulder
and head silhouette of the humanoid security patrol robot (CUSTODIA-2: no LEDs, no
logo, head turned three degrees) — barely distinguishable, hovering as a dark
presence in the deep-blue interior.

LIGHTING: Single cold cobalt backlight from the storm sky behind the cleaner +
single warm amber pilot light from inside the office that catches the rose's
charcoal edges. M2 cobalto tormenta modality with extreme contrast.

PALETTE: cyan-cobalt #1E2A3A dominant on the frost, deep black charcoal on the rose,
single warm amber highlight on the rose's edges where the office light catches it,
near-black graphite for the robot silhouette behind glass.

NO text. NO Asian characters. NO neon. NO LEDs. The macro proximity makes city
backdrop irrelevant — only frost, rose, reflection, and out-of-focus interior shadow.
Painterly oil-paint texture with visible charcoal strokes on the rose. NO digital
painting sheen. NO abstract overlays.

Test 120px: the rose must read as a flower silhouette even at thumbnail size, with
the cleaner reflection as secondary element and the robot as tertiary presence.
```
